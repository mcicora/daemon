Daemon Crossed the Rainbow Bridge on March 20th, 2026

For 13 years, Daemon was my rock and bridged so many significant parts of my life: 3 apartments, a house, 2 partners, 2 other dogs, 2 states, personal losses, a global pandemic. He was always there.

Despite his size, he was a ninja, appearing and disappearing from a room without anyone even noticing. He was stubborn and demanding but also empathic.

Though never the loudest dog, it was his presence that was the loudest. The house will be quieter without him.

He was loved and will be missed. I already miss him.
